---
name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, says "grill me", or before starting a complex feature.
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding.

Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one.

For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time, waiting for feedback before continuing.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead of asking.

When all branches are resolved, summarize the decisions made and suggest next steps.
